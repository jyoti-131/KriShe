About Krishe:Crop Recommendation System

Empowering farmers with data-driven decisions for optimal crop selection and sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

At Krishe, we're on a mission to revolutionize agriculture through cutting-edge machine learning technology. Our goal is to provide accurate, timely crop recommendations that optimize yields, promote sustainability, and support food security worldwide.

How Krishe Works

Krishe utilizes a sophisticated machine learning model trained on extensive agricultural data. By analyzing key environmental parameters such as soil composition, climate conditions, and historical data, our algorithm predicts the most suitable crop for your specific conditions.
